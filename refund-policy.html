Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time by emailing info@nuvoraweb.co.za. There are no cancellation fees. Your cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and you retain full access until that date. After cancellation your website remains live for 30 days while you arrange migration.

Refunds

Because our services are digital and delivered immediately, we do not offer refunds for the current billing period once commenced.

Exceptions

We will consider a refund in the following cases: a technical failure on our part preventing service delivery reported within 7 days; duplicate charges due to billing error; cancellation within 24 hours of first payment before work has commenced. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

Service Delivery Guarantee

We commit to delivering your first website draft within 5 working days of receiving all required assets. If we fail to meet this, you are entitled to a one-month credit on your next invoice.

Ad Spend

For Dominance package clients, ad spend paid directly to Google or Meta is subject to those platforms' own refund policies. Nuvora does not hold or process ad spend funds.

Contact

Billing queries: info@nuvoraweb.co.za. We respond within one business day.
