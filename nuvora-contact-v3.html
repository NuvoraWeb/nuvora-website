Common questions

Things people ask
before getting in touch.

How long does a website take to build?
You will see a first draft within 48 hours of your discovery call. Full launch typically happens within 5–10 business days depending on feedback speed.
Is there a setup fee?
Founding clients (first 10) pay no setup fee. Standard clients pay a once-off setup fee based on their package. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
Do I own my website?
Yes. If you cancel, you take your site files and domain with you. We do not hold your digital assets hostage.
What if I already have a website?
We can rebuild it or work with what you have. We will assess honestly which approach makes more sense for your goals.
Do you work with businesses outside SA?
Our focus is South Africa but we can work with any business that invoices in ZAR. Drop us a line and we will chat.
What payment methods do you accept?
We bill monthly via Paystack — card, EFT, and instant EFT all supported. Setup fees are billed once upfront.
