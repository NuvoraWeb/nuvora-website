Common questions

Everything you need
to know before you start.

How quickly will my site be live?
Our standard turnaround is 48 hours from receiving your information and sign-off. We've never missed this deadline. You'll get a preview link before anything goes live.
Do I need to provide photos and content?
Ideally yes — your own photos always perform best. If you don't have them, we'll use professional stock imagery and write all your copy for you based on a short questionnaire.
Can I cancel or change packages?
Yes to both. No minimum contract. Cancel with 30 days notice. Upgrade or downgrade anytime — changes take effect on your next billing date.
What's included in the monthly fee?
Hosting, SSL, security, updates, your allocated changes, chatbot, email flows, and all the tools that run your digital system. No hidden costs. No surprise invoices.
How does billing work?
We use Paystack for secure monthly billing in ZAR. Your card is charged on the same date each month. No forex exposure, no dollar-billing surprises.
Do I own my website?
Yes — you own all your content and your domain. If you ever leave, we'll give you the full code and help you migrate. No hostage situations here.
Do you work with businesses outside Johannesburg?
Absolutely. We work with businesses across South Africa — Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria, Bloemfontein. Everything is done remotely via WhatsApp, email, and video call.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
We keep revising until you're satisfied before going live. Post-launch, each package includes a set number of change rounds per month — and we'll always fix genuine errors immediately.
