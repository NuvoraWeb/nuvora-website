Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Nuvora is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

We collect contact information (name, email, phone, business name) submitted via our forms or chatbot, business information shared during onboarding, usage data via Google Analytics, and payment information processed securely via Paystack.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver our services, send service-related communications, respond to enquiries, process payments, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Lead and contact data is stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain data for the duration of your subscription plus 12 months after cancellation.

5. Email Communications

By submitting your details, you consent to receiving follow-up emails about our services. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You may opt out at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics, Paystack, Resend, Supabase, and Anthropic. Each has their own privacy policy governing their data processing.

7. Your Rights (POPIA)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights contact info@nuvoraweb.co.za.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and functionality. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.
